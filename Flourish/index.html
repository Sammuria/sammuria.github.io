Exercise 1: Brand Audit Questionnaire

✎

What are your key offerings/products/services? Has this changed over the last 12 months?

⌚

Who is your key audience? What are they like, and what do they believe in?

⌚

⌚

⌚

What isn’t working / what are some things you are currently finding challenging?

⌚

⌚
